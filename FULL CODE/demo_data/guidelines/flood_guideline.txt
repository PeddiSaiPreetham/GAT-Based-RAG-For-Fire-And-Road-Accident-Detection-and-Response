FLOOD RESPONSE GUIDELINES

Immediate Actions:
1. Move to higher ground immediately.
2. Disconnect electricity if safe to do so.
3. Avoid walking or driving through flood waters.

Do Not:
- Do not go near moving water; 15 cm depth can knock you down.
- Do not use contaminated tap water.
- Do not enter flooded basements due to electrocution risk.

Critical Information:
- Monitor official advisories.
- Carry emergency kit: water, medication, flashlight.
- Secure important documents in waterproof bags.
