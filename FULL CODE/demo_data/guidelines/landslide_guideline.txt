LANDSLIDE INCIDENT RESPONSE

Immediate Actions:
1. Evacuate slope areas and move to stable ground.
2. Watch for sudden ground movement, cracks, or trees tilting.
3. Warn others nearby.

Do Not:
- Do not return to the slide area.
- Do not cross active landslide slopes.

Critical Information:
- Report blocked roads and broken utilities.
- Monitor rainfall warnings.
- Follow directives from geological authorities.
