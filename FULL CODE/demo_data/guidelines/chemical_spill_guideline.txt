CHEMICAL SPILL / INDUSTRIAL LEAK GUIDELINES

Immediate Actions:
1. Move upwind and uphill.
2. Avoid breathing fumes; cover nose and mouth with cloth.
3. Evacuate the area and isolate hazard zone.

Do Not:
- Do not touch spilled material.
- Do not eat, drink, or smoke near the spill.

Critical Information:
- Identify placards or labels if possible (from a safe distance).
- Notify hazardous material response team.
- Remove contaminated clothing immediately.
