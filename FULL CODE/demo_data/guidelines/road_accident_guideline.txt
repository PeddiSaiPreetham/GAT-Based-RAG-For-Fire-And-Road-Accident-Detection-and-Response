ROAD TRAFFIC ACCIDENT RESPONSE

Immediate Actions:
1. Secure area and prevent secondary collisions.
2. Turn off vehicle ignitions.
3. Provide first aid if trained.

Do Not:
- Do not move seriously injured persons unnecessarily.
- Do not stand in active lanes.

Critical Information:
- Call emergency medical services.
- Note vehicle numbers, direction of travel.
- Look for leaking fuel or fire hazard.
