STRUCTURAL COLLAPSE RESPONSE

Immediate Actions:
1. Evacuate building immediately.
2. Warn others about unsafe areas.
3. Stay clear of walls, beams, and overhead objects.

Do Not:
- Do not re-enter collapsed structures.
- Do not disturb debris unnecessarily.

Critical Information:
- Look for trapped individuals.
- Provide rescue teams with floor plans if available.
- Monitor for aftershocks (if earthquake-related).
