FIRE INCIDENT GUIDELINES

Immediate Actions:
1. Raise alarm and alert occupants.
2. Evacuate through nearest exit; avoid elevators.
3. Close doors behind you to slow fire spread.
4. Stay low to avoid smoke inhalation.

Do Not:
- Do not attempt to open a hot door.
- Do not re-enter the building once outside.
- Do not use water on electrical or oil fires.

Critical Information:
- Notify fire department immediately.
- Assist children, elderly, and injured persons.
- Report location of trapped individuals.

If clothing catches fire:
- STOP, DROP, and ROLL.
