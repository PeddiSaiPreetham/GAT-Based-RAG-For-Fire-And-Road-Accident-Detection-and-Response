EXPLOSION RESPONSE SOP

Immediate Actions:
1. Take cover behind solid objects.
2. Evacuate area due to risk of secondary explosions.
3. Look for fires, falling debris, and structural instability.

Do Not:
- Do not use mobile phones near gas leaks.
- Do not move injured persons unless in immediate danger.

Critical Information:
- Report gas smell, fire, or loud detonations.
- Secure the area and prevent entry.
